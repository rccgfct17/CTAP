About RCCG Christ the Anchor Parish

Learn about our mission, vision, and the foundation of our faith

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To make heaven. To take as many people with us. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our History

Founded in 1952 by Pa Josiah Akindayomi, RCCG has grown from a small fellowship in Lagos, Nigeria to a global ministry present in 197 countries with over 9 million members and 50,000 parishes worldwide. We are committed to fulfilling God's covenant to take the world for Jesus Christ.

Our Values

Holiness: Our lifestyle and foundation
Obedience: To God's Word and calling
Faithfulness: In service and covenant
Excellence: In all we do for God
Love: For God and humanity

Who We Are

We are part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), founded in 1952 by Pa Josiah Akindayomi in Lagos, Nigeria. What began as a small fellowship called "The Glory of God Fellowship" has grown into a global ministry with presence in 197 countries, over 9 million members, and more than 50,000 parishes worldwide. Christ the Anchor Parish operates under FCT Province 17 in Abuja, Nigeria, as part of this worldwide family of believers.

Three Pillars of Our Faith

Word Based

We believe that the entire Scripture, both Old and New Testament are written by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit. All Christian teachings and attitudes are established in the Holy Bible. The Bible is the written and revealed Will of God.

Prayer Focused

Prayer is the foundation of all our activities. We believe in the power of prayer to change situations and lives. Our famous Holy Ghost Service, held monthly, draws hundreds of thousands seeking spiritual breakthrough.

Mission Driven

Our mission is to plant churches worldwide and reach every nation for Jesus Christ. We are committed to having a member of RCCG in every family of all nations, pursuing this objective until every nation is reached.

Our Ministry Focus

Christ the Anchor Parish is part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God worldwide family, committed to fulfilling the covenant established by God with our founder, Pa Josiah Akindayomi. We are dedicated to making heaven, taking as many people with us as possible, and planting churches to reach every nation for Jesus Christ.

Our parish offers various programs including:

  • Sunday Worship Services - For praise, worship, and ministry of God's Word
  • Holy Ghost Service - Monthly all-night miracle service following RCCG tradition
  • Bible Studies & Exposition - For deeper understanding of Scripture and spiritual growth
  • Prayer & Deliverance Sessions - For spiritual breakthrough and freedom through prayer
  • Youth & Children Ministries - Developing the next generation of believers
  • Women & Men Fellowships - Gender-specific discipleship and outreach programs
  • Evangelism & Missions - Spreading the Gospel and planting new churches
  • Community Outreach - Serving the poor and needy in our locality
  • Online Giving & Partnership - Supporting the work of God financially

As part of RCCG's global vision, we are committed to holiness as our lifestyle and excellence in all our endeavors, pursuing the objective of reaching every nation until Jesus Christ returns in glory.
