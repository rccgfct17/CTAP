Get Involved

We have multiple ministries where you can serve, grow, and make an impact

Ushering Ministry

Welcome and guide visitors with warmth and excellence. Ensure sanctuary comfort and organization.

Praise & Worship Team

Lead the congregation in worship through music, dance, and creative expressions of faith.

Children's Ministry

Nurture and teach children in the ways of Christ through age-appropriate programs and activities.

Youth Ministry

Empower young people to become leaders who impact their generation for Christ.

Women's Ministry

Build strong Christian women who are rooted in faith and impact their families and communities.

Men's Fellowship

Develop mature Christian men who serve as spiritual leaders in their homes and society.

Media & Publishing

Communicate God's message through various media platforms and published materials.

Outreach Ministry

Reach the lost with the Gospel and serve the community with practical help and love.

Explore Each Ministry

Ushering Ministry

Purpose: To provide warm welcomes and excellent service to all visitors and members.

Responsibilities: Greeting visitors, directing seating, managing facilities, maintaining order.

Requirements: Friendly disposition, reliability, good communication skills, punctuality.

Contact: Speak to any usher or visit the office

Praise & Worship Team

Purpose: Lead congregational worship and create a spirit of praise.

Responsibilities: Song selection, leading worship, coordinating musicians and dancers.

Requirements: Musical ability (or willingness to learn), spiritual maturity, commitment.

Contact: Speak to the worship leader

Children's Ministry

Purpose: Provide Christian education and mentoring to children.

Responsibilities: Teaching Bible lessons, organizing activities, pastoral care.

Requirements: Love for children, patience, creativity, biblical knowledge.

Contact: Speak to the children's ministry leader

Youth Ministry

Purpose: Develop young people spiritually and equip them as future leaders.

Responsibilities: Youth programs, mentoring, organizing events, spiritual guidance.

Requirements: Understanding of youth culture, mentoring ability, passion for young people.

Contact: Speak to the youth minister

Women's Ministry

Purpose: Build strong Christian women and support their spiritual growth.

Responsibilities: Women's programs, Bible studies, community service, mentoring.

Requirements: Spiritual maturity, leadership ability, commitment to women's development.

Contact: Speak to the women's ministry coordinator

Men's Fellowship

Purpose: Develop spiritual leaders among men.

Responsibilities: Men's meetings, leadership training, community impact projects.

Requirements: Leadership quality, spiritual maturity, commitment to discipleship.

Contact: Speak to the men's fellowship leader
