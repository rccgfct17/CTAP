Types of Giving

Understanding different forms of contributions

Tithes (10%)

A tithe is one-tenth of your income given to support the church. It's a biblical principle of honoring God with the first fruits of your labor.

Offerings

Offerings are voluntary gifts given above your tithe. They're given in response to God's blessings and support specific projects or needs.

Seed Offerings

Special offerings sown with the faith that God will multiply them back to you. They're usually given for specific prayer requests or purposes.

Prayer Offerings

Given specifically for intercession, deliverance, or prayer requests. These funds support our prayer ministry and special prayer sessions.

Benevolence Giving

Contributions given to support the needy, sick, and less fortunate members of our congregation and community.

Project Giving

Contributions for specific church projects including facility maintenance, construction, equipment, or ministry programs.
