About Our Parish

RCCG Christ The Anchor Parish FCT Province 17 is a vibrant community of believers committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God worldwide ministry.

Our mission is to anchor souls in Christ through sound biblical teaching, passionate worship, and compassionate service to our community.

We believe in the transformative power of God's love and are dedicated to raising disciples who will impact their world for Christ.
