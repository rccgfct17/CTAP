About Our Parish

Our Mission & Vision

RCCG Christ the Anchor Parish is part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, FCT Province 17. We are committed to making heaven, taking as many people with us as possible, and having a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

Our vision is to be a multi-ethnic, multi-generational church that is committed to evangelism, discipleship, and raising leaders who will transform their communities for Christ.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit. Our church is a place where everyone is welcome, regardless of background or circumstance.
