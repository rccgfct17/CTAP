About Our Parish

Discover our mission, vision, and the values that guide our ministry in the Federal Capital Territory.

RCCG Christ The Anchor Parish, FCT Province 17

We are a vibrant parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) located in the heart of the Federal Capital Territory, committed to being an anchor for souls in a turbulent world.

Our mission is to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, make disciples, and equip believers for spiritual growth and service. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

Under the leadership of our Parish Pastor, we strive to create an atmosphere where God's presence is tangible, where families are strengthened, and where individuals discover their God-given purpose.
