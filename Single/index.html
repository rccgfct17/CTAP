Our Foundation

Our Mission

To make heaven. To take as many people with us. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our History

Founded in 1952 by Pa Josiah Akindayomi, RCCG has grown from a small fellowship in Lagos, Nigeria to a global ministry with presence in 197 countries, over 9 million members, and more than 50,000 parishes worldwide. We are committed to fulfilling God's covenant to take the world for Jesus Christ.

Our Values

Faithfulness: In service and covenant

About RCCG Christ the Anchor Parish

We are part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), a Pentecostal church with a worldwide presence. Christ the Anchor Parish operates under FCT Province 17 in Abuja, Nigeria. Our parish is dedicated to:

Three Pillars of Our Faith

Word Based

Rooted firmly in the scriptures and the undiluted teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe in the power of God's Word to transform and sustain us.

Prayer Focused

Believing in the power of prayer to change situations and lives. Prayer is the foundation of all our activities and spiritual breakthroughs.

Love Filled

A community where everyone is welcome, loved, and valued. We demonstrate Christ's love through our actions and genuine care for one another.

Who We Are

Christ the Anchor Parish is a vibrant community of believers united in worship and service. We are committed to spreading the Gospel, fostering spiritual growth, and serving our community with compassion and excellence.

Our parish offers various programs including:

  • Sunday Worship Services - For praise, worship, and ministry of God's Word
  • Bible Studies & Exposition - For deeper understanding of Scripture
  • Prayer & Deliverance Sessions - For spiritual breakthrough and freedom
  • Youth Programs - Mentoring and developing young leaders
  • Women & Men Ministries - Gender-specific discipleship and outreach
  • Community Outreach - Serving the poor and needy in our locality
