About Our Parish

Get to know Christ the Anchor Parish

Our Vision & Mission

Christ the Anchor Parish is a vibrant community of believers committed to making heaven and taking as many people with us as possible. We are dedicated to raising disciples for Christ through the teaching of God's Word, fellowship, and evangelism.

As part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) worldwide, we uphold the vision of our General Overseer, Pastor E.A. Adeboye, to spread the gospel to all nations.

Our parish in FCT Province 17 serves the Abuja community with various programs and ministries designed to meet spiritual needs at every stage of life.

Our Services

Join us for worship and fellowship

Sunday Worship Service

7:30 AM - 10:30 AM

Our main weekly service featuring uplifting worship, prayer, and an impactful message from God's Word. Holy Communion is served on the first Sunday of each month.

Tuesday Bible Study

5:30 PM - 7:00 PM

An in-depth study of God's Word to help believers grow in knowledge and understanding of Scripture. This midweek service strengthens faith and provides practical biblical teaching.

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

A powerful time of intercession and prayer for personal needs, the church, our nation, and global concerns. Experience the transforming power of prayer in this weekly gathering.
